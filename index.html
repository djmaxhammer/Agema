Welcome!

We would like to introduce a new line of AGEMA Super Doublet series apochromatic refraсtors: 130, 150 and 180 mm diameter. The design is based on two lenses with a wide air gap and “flit in front” configuration. The first lens made from high quality, fine annialed optical glass. The second lens made from a Fluorite single block crystal. All the elements have anti-reflection coating. Our lenses have the high level of color correction; in visual 430 — 706nm range, they give a diffraction quality image. Polychromatic Strehl number exceeds 90% of the specified range. The color correction for wave aberrations is 2.5 — 3.5 times better than the criterion Abbe in use for apochromatic lens . We are taking into the account all the changing glass melt to melt factors to save the high rated quality image. Our SD-series apochromatic refractors deliver the pinpoint sharpness, clarity and high definition star images. Visually on the Moon and planet there is no color halo even at the highest powers. All the instruments include our high quality two-speed ridged focuser with collet lock and drum-type pinion shaft brake, special cell with fine lens adjustment, aluminum tube equipped with knife-type diaphragms, machined from solid tube rings and dovetail plate, and 40 mm format field corrector that gives diffraction quality image.
